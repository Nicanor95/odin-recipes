Fettuccine Alfredo

Original Author: ERINMARIE

This rich and creamy fettuccine Alfredo is an easy recipe even for beginner cooks, but it's impressive enough for company.

Ingredients

  • Pasta: Fettuccine pasta.
  • Butter: Two sticks.
  • Cream: A cup
  • Seasonings: Usually salt, pepper, and garlic salt.
  • Cheese: Romano & Parmesan

Steps

So you got your ingredients, here's what you do:

  1. Cook the pasta.
  2. Melt the butter and cream on the stove, season and stir in the cheese.
  3. Toss the pasta in the sauce.
