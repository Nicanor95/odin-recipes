Pesto Pasta

Original Author: Lauren

This top-rated pesto pasta recipe, which comes together in just 15 minutes, is the perfect quick and easy weeknight dinner.

Ingredients

  • Pasta: Any you like.
  • Onion: 1/2 cup chopped
  • Pesto: 2 1/2 tablespoons
  • Seasonings: Salt, black pepper
  • Cheese: Parmesan

Steps

So you got your ingredients, here's what you do:

  1. Cook the pasta in salted water.
  2. Cook the onion in oil until translucent, then stir in the pesto and seasonings.
  3. Add the mixture to the pasta and toss with cheese.
